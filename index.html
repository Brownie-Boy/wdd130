Idaho

Idaho sits in the north western part of the United States. It borders several states such as Wyoming, Montana, Washington, Oregon, Nevada, and Utah. Idaho also borders the country of Canada to the North. Idaho is well known for its beautiful mountains and valleys. Tourists love to go visit all of the national parks and old ghost towns. Idaho is also a big recreational state, where people from all over come to go motorbiking, boating, and hiking.
