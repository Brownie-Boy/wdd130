Overview

Purpose

To provide the best whitewater rafting adventures to suit people from every age group, rafting experience level, and comfort level.

Audience

White water rafting is for any adventure-seeking individuals and groups who are passionate about outdoor activities and are looking for thrilling rafting experiences in diverse natural settings. Not Sure what to wear? Come in swim suits and clothes that dry quickly, preferably not cotton. Flip flops are not allowed, so please make sure to wear shoes that stay put. Any safety concerns or worries our river guides can help! They are all trained and experenced in handling emergency situations. Finally don't worry about having to find shuttles or rafts. We provide rafts for you and have shuttles that can pick you up at the end of the ride and take you back to the parking lot.
